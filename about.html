ABOUT US

'Clean India Icon' takes a step towards the 'Swachh Bharat Abhiyan' that was started by our Hon'ble Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi. We aim to make India greener & cleaner by helping the citizens who are willing to make a change by providing a platform where they can turn their ideas to reality.


We, at Clean India Icon render help to all the responsible and determined citizens who want work for a cause. You can help in the following ways:

* Organise Events and we will publish your events and help you get volunteers.

* Donate for a cause (You can either donate for a particular event by visiting the 'explore' page)

* You can also Exchange old e-waste or waste that is hazardous to our environment. Just give us details and we will be at your door step to collect it.

* Volunteer for the events and be a responsible citizen. Help our comunitee grow
